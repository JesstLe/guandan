## Research Principle

Guandan is not positioned as a card-game product. It is used as a dense testbed for:

- imperfect information,
- zero explicit communication,
- two-vs-two cooperation and competition,
- dynamic legal action spaces,
- rule-verifiable actions and explanations,
- implicit signaling through public actions.

The paper should make claims about verifiable multi-agent reasoning, not about building a stronger Guandan bot alone.
